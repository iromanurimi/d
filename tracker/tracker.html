Lokacin Haihuwa Ya Wuce

Ranar haihuwar da kika shigar ta wuce. Wannan yana nufin cewa watakila an riga an haifi jariri, ko kuma ana jiran haihuwa a kowane lokaci.

Abubuwan da za ka iya yi:

  • Tuntuɓi likita ko ungozoma don shawarwari
  • Yi gwajin ciki idan baka taba yin haka ba
  • Duba alamomin haihuwa kamar fashewar ciki ko ruwan ciki
  • Shirya kayan haihuwa da kayan jariri
